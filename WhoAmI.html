Preparing for a homestead:

  1. Learn as much as you can. It is far more efficient and cost-effective to have a detailed plan before you begin building.
    • What do you want your homesteading life to look like? Do you aim for complete or partial self-reliance?
    • What produce do you hope to grow? Will you have the space?
    • Will you have animals? How much space will they need?
  2. What zone do I live in? Will it be compatible with the plants I want to grow, or will I need a greenhouse? (skip to bottom)
